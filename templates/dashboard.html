About Us

Credit Card Fraud Detection Website is a modern web application that leverages advanced machine learning models to detect fraudulent credit card transactions in real-time. Our platform combines the power of Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM, and Logistic Regression in a robust ensemble, providing high accuracy and low false positives. With a user-friendly interface, secure authentication, and real-time analytics, we aim to help users and organizations protect themselves from financial fraud efficiently and effectively.
